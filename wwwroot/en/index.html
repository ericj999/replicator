One click solution to backup or copy files between devices

File Replicator is a one way file replication tool that copies files from one device to another device. It is easy to use. Simply create a task. All the files will be copied to designated destination when you run the task first time. After that, the files created after the previous run will be copied to the designated location when you run the task again. It maintains a list of execution statuses, so you will know when the status of the tasks. You can use this software to backup your photos from your phones or tablets to your PC hard disks. You don't need to pay for the cloud storage. You can easily manage all the replications from multiple devices on your PC.

Product Features

Backup Photos

Backup photos from portable devices to local storages on PC. So you can free up the storage on the portable devices and also manage or view your photos on your PC.

Transfer Music

Transfer your favorite music albums from your PC to the portable devices such as smart phones or MP3 player.*

Backup Files

Backups file from a folder to another folder and maintains the same the folder structures.
